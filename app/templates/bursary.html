Amazon Recruitment Bursary, Amazon Web Services, located in Johannesburg.

In 2018, the Amazon Development Centre (South Africa) established the bursary programme in order to develop a talent pipeline for Amazon (AWS) within the country. The bursary will provide cover for the following expenses: tuition fees, prescribed books, stationery, accommodation in residence or equivalent private accommodation, meals, transport and a personal computer, up to a maximum amount. Careers covered in the programme may include: Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Information Technology.

AAA Electrum Academic Bursary, Electrum, located in Cape Town.

The bursary provides cover for: tuition fees (for final year), textbooks, new laptop and accommodation. The bursary also provides the bursary recipient with an internship position and mentorship. Students are required to work for the company for 1 year after completing their studies. Careers covered in the programme may include: Computer Science, Computer Engineering

Nedbank Bursary, Nedbank, located in Johannesburg

Each year, Nedbank awards bursaries to students in financial need who are studying or wish to study towards an undergraduate degree or diploma qualification, but are lacking the financial means to do so. Bursaries will be awarded within scare skills fields, and selection will be based on academic performance and financial need. The Nedbank bursary will provide coverage for tuition fees, prescribed textbooks, meals, accommodation and a monthly allowance to cover additional study related expenses.
